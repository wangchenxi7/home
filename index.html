About me

I'm a postdoc in UCLA working with Dr. Harry Xu. I got my PhD degree in Institute of Computing Techonology, Chinese Academy of Science in 2018, under the supervision of Dr. Xiaobing Feng. I got my Bachelor degree in Tianjin University in 2013. My research interests span managed runtime, memory management, big data systems and emerging hardwares, such as non-volatile memory.
